    Serving Duluth Families with Heart

    Local Commitment & Quality Care

    Homecare Solutions is proud to be a trusted partner for families throughout Duluth, Georgia. As a locally rooted agency, we understand the community we serve — from the close-knit neighborhoods near Chattahoochee Pointe to the vibrant developments along Pleasant Hill Road. Our caregivers aren't just professionals; many of them live right here in Gwinnett County and share your neighbors' values of family, respect, and community.

    We believe quality care starts with accountability. Every caregiver on our team is personally interviewed, thoroughly screened, and continuously trained to uphold the highest standards of in-home support. When a Homecare Solutions caregiver enters your home, you can feel confident they've earned that trust.

    Personalized In-Home Care Plans

    No two people age in exactly the same way, and no two care plans should look alike. When you contact Homecare Solutions, a care coordinator will visit your home — at no cost or obligation — to conduct a full assessment. We listen to your loved one's preferences, review any physician recommendations, and collaborate with your family to build a plan that fits your life, not someone else's template.

    Our services in Duluth include:

    • Personal care & hygiene assistance
    • Companionship & social engagement
    • Dementia & Alzheimer's support
    • Post-hospital transition care
    • Respite care for family caregivers
    • 24-hour & overnight care
    • Meal preparation & nutrition support
    • Light housekeeping & errands

    Expert & Compassionate Caregivers

    Every member of our caregiving team passes a rigorous multi-step vetting process that includes a criminal background check, reference verification, and a structured skills assessment. But we go beyond credentials — we look for the warmth, patience, and reliability that you can't teach in a classroom. Caregiver training is ongoing, ensuring our team stays current with best practices in senior care and safety.

    Why Families in Duluth Choose Us

    Families across Duluth turn to Homecare Solutions because we treat every client the way we'd want our own family treated — with kindness, attentiveness, and genuine care. Our care coordinators remain involved throughout the client relationship, checking in regularly to make sure the care plan is working and adjusting it whenever life changes.

    Whether your loved one needs a few hours of companion care each week or full-time support following a hospital stay, Homecare Solutions is here to help you navigate the journey with confidence.

      Rooted in Community, Driven by Purpose

      Homecare Solutions was founded in Duluth, Georgia with a simple mission: to provide the kind of in-home care that we'd want for our own families. What started as a small team of dedicated caregivers has grown into one of Gwinnett County's most trusted home care agencies — without losing the personal touch that sets us apart.

      Our founders recognized a gap in the market: too many agencies treated clients as care recipients rather than individuals. We built Homecare Solutions to be different — an agency where care coordinators genuinely know their clients, where caregivers are matched thoughtfully, and where families feel heard at every step.

      Today, we serve over 500 families across Duluth, Suwanee, Johns Creek, Lawrenceville, Norcross, Peachtree Corners, Sugar Hill, Buford, and beyond.

      • Dignity: Every interaction honors the whole person
      • Reliability: We show up, every time, as promised
      • Transparency: Clear communication with clients and families
      • Excellence: Continuous training and quality improvement
